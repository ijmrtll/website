About Me

I have 5+ years of experience teaching and implementing practical concepts of electrical engineering in topics related to: Digital and Analog Electronics, Industrial Automation, Robotics and Control Theory.

Along with a wide range of technical skills, I am also versed in design theory and have an eye for aesthetic designs.

My passion is knowing how things work, from simple electronics to complex mechatronics/robotics systems. My interests lay in entrepreneurship, intrapreneurship, and I'm an advocate of the maker movement.
